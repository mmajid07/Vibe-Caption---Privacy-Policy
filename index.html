Privacy Policy — Vibe Caption

Last updated: June 2026

Vibe Caption ("the app", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. Vibe Caption is an offline application: it does not have user accounts, does not connect to any server, and does not collect or transmit any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no crash-reporting SDKs, and makes no network requests.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All app data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Generated caption history (most recent 30)
  • Saved (favorited) captions
  • Preferences: theme, default niche, default mood
  • Local counter for in-app share rewards

This data is stored locally and removed when you uninstall the app or clear it manually.

3. Photos and Media

With your permission, the app can read selected images and save generated caption images to your gallery. All processing happens on-device and nothing is uploaded.

4. Permissions We Request

Permission Purpose
Read images / storage Select photo as caption background
Write storage (Android ≤ 9) Save caption image to gallery

We do not request location, microphone, camera, contacts, or video access.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not collect or share any data. When you use the share feature, your device handles it directly via system share sheet.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Data Security

All data remains on your device. There is no server or remote storage.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will appear in the app settings.
